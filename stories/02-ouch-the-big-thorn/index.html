Ouch! The Big Thorn

A story about friendship, a truck, and a very brave squirrel doctor

🐰 Rabbit 🐘 Elephant 🐿️ Dr. Squirrel
Page 1

One sunny morning, Rabbit and Elephant were walking down the road together.

Rabbit was in the middle of a very exciting story — waving her arms, making big faces, and talking very fast.

Elephant listened with all of his attention, his big ears perked up, not missing a single word.

Page 2

Rabbit kept talking. Elephant kept listening. Neither of them noticed the great big thorn sitting right in the middle of the road.

OUCH!

Elephant's foot came straight down on it. He let out a huge "OOOH!" and sat down on the spot, his leg throbbing with pain.

Page 3

Rabbit stopped mid-sentence. "Elephant! Are you okay?!" She saw the thorn and her eyes went wide.

"Don't move!" she said firmly. "I'll get help!"

And with that, she turned around and RAN — faster than she had ever run before — all the way back to their house.

Page 4

At home was their big truck. The driving cab was tiny — just big enough for Rabbit — but the cargo area at the back? Enormous.

It wasn't graceful getting Elephant in. There was a lot of grunting. But they did it!

Then Rabbit climbed into the cab and drove at FULL SPEED to the hospital.

Page 5

At the hospital, a small squirrel in a bright white coat came rushing out the moment she heard the truck.

"Emergency! Right this way!" called Dr. Squirrel briskly.

She took one look at Elephant's leg, clicked into action, and wheeled him straight to the emergency room.

Page 6

Dr. Squirrel worked carefully and quickly. First she cleaned all around the thorn.

Then — using her special long tweezers — she gripped the thorn tight and pulled.

POP!

It was enormous! Even Dr. Squirrel raised her eyebrows. Then she stitched up the wound neatly, wrapped it in a clean white bandage, and said, "All done!"

Page 7

Elephant was moved to a cosy ward room to rest. He had a soft bed, a window with a view of the garden, and a clean white bandage on his leg.

Dr. Squirrel looked at her clipboard. "Three days of rest — no running, no planting, no adventures!"

Elephant gave a small nod. He was already feeling better.

Page 8

Every single day, Rabbit came to visit. She brought food, funny books, and her very best silly stories.

On Day 1, Elephant said "Ouch."

On Day 2, Elephant said "Hmm."

On Day 3, Elephant was laughing so hard at Rabbit's story that his bandage nearly came loose!

Page 9

On the morning of Day 4, Dr. Squirrel walked in with a big smile. "You're all healed up! You may go home."

Elephant beamed. Rabbit beamed even more.

They thanked Dr. Squirrel many, many times — until she (laughing) had to kindly wave them both out of the door.

Page 10

Rabbit pulled the big truck round to the entrance, and Elephant climbed into the cargo area — just like before, but this time with a big smile on his face.

Rabbit drove them home slowly and carefully. No more thorns. No more ouch.

Just two friends, a bumpy truck, and the long road home. ✨ The End ✨
